Selling on the BUYSELL app is simple and can be done in a few easy steps!

The first step is to create a listing by clicking on the camera (SELL) icon located at the bottom of your home screen. Then simply fill in your Listing Details from the top of the screen and in order to the bottom.

  1. Select the Store you'd like to sell your item in*

  2. Add photos of your item

  3. Add a photo of the size/brand tag inside your item

  4. Create a Headline (Title) describing your item in a few words

  5. Further describe important item details by pointing out things like flaws, fabric content, and measurements

  6. Select the Category your item belongs to

  7. Select the Size of your item

  8. Enter the item Brand

  9. Enter the item color

  10. Choose the condition of your item

  11. Enter the price you want to sell your item for

  12. Select the weight and cost of shipping your item

  13. Post your item!

*Store descriptions can be found by clicking the information link above the Store icons on the Listing Details screen **Save Draft feature is still in development and will be available soon!
Selling Fee

After your item sells, we will deduct a fee from the final order price.

For sales under $10, the fee is a flat rate of $2.25.

For sales above $10, the fee is 20% and you keep 80%.

What can I sell?

BUYSELL is a marketplace for new and secondhand fashion and accessories for men and women. Please refrain from listing items that do not fall under the categories currently available on BUYSELL. All items must be clearly and accurately represented. BUYSELL strictly prohibits the sale of illegal items, including but not limited to, replicas or fakes, products derived from threatened or extinct species, anything requiring a prescription, medical products / items making medical claims (for example N95 masks), food, supplements, drugs, drug paraphernalia, tobacco products, vapes, smoke instruments & accessories, live animals, and stolen goods. Items that promote or display violence, discrimination, hatred, and crime are also prohibited. Members who buy, list, or otherwise attempt to sell such illegal items risk immediate and permanent suspension from BUYSELL.
BUYSELL also prohibits the sale of items that cannot be shipped using our shipping label types per USPS regulations, including nail polish, perfumes, aerosols, and lithium batteries on their own. Visit USPS for more information on what cannot be shipped. (https://www.usps.com/ship/shipping-restrictions.htm)
